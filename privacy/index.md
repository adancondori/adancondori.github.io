*Last updated: 2026-06-20*

This is a personal blog hosted on [GitHub Pages](https://pages.github.com/). It does not require an account, accept payments, or store personal data on a server I control. I try to keep the data footprint small.

## What I (and my providers) collect

- **Hosting (GitHub Pages):** GitHub serves this site over HTTPS and may log standard request data (IP address, user-agent, referrer) per its [Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-general-privacy-statement).
- **Analytics:** I use Google Analytics / Google Tag Manager to understand which posts are read and how visitors arrive. Analytics use cookies and may collect IP-derived approximate location, device type, and browsing behavior on this site. See [Google's privacy policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Comments (Disqus):** Some posts use [Disqus](https://disqus.com/) for comments. If you choose to comment, Disqus collects whatever you submit (name, email, profile) under [their privacy policy](https://help.disqus.com/en/articles/1717103-disqus-privacy-policy). You can read the site without commenting.
- **CDN (Fastly):** GitHub Pages uses Fastly as a CDN; standard CDN access logs apply.

## What I do not collect

- I do not sell, share, or rent any data.
- I do not run remarketing pixels, fingerprinting scripts, or third-party ad networks.
- I do not require accounts, logins, or payment information.

## Cookies

This site uses cookies for analytics and (if you interact) Disqus. You can block cookies at the browser level without losing site functionality — the only thing that breaks is comments.

## Your choices

- Block cookies in your browser, or use a privacy extension. The site will work fine.
- Opt out of Google Analytics with the [official browser add-on](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout).
- Contact me to request deletion of any comment you posted: adan.condoric@gmail.com.

## Children

Content here targets a professional engineering audience and is not directed at children under 13.

## Changes

If I change this policy, I update the date above. Material changes will be noted on the site.
